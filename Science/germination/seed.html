EXPERIMENT-3

Blooming seed

Supplies Needed :

  • 1 pea seed
  • 1 tissue
  • 1 zip bag
  • Water

Procedure:

Step 1 – Damp the tissue with water
Step 2 - Place the tissue in zip bag and add seed to it . Zip the bag and keep it in warm place
Step 3 - Keep sprinking water whenever the tissue dries .
Step 4 - After 2-3 days yo will see roots coming out from seed.
Step 5 - Place the seed in the pot with soil and in sunlight

Conclusion

Germination! That means the plant is sprouting it’s roots
